Ingredients

  • Dough

    • Steps

    1. Working one at a time, using your fingers press out a piece of dough into a rectangular shape that is the same width as the sausages. Place the sausage on top and roll, then pinch where the dough meets. Place this seam side down onto a large baking sheet. Place kolaches about 1 inch apart from each other. Continue until all dough and sausages have been rolled.
